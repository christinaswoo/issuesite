LET'S TAKE ACTION



In June 2022, the U.S. Supreme Court overturned Roe v. Wade and eliminated the constitutional right to abortion. This devastating ruling paved the way for states across the country to ban abortion outright—and half the U.S. states are expected take such action.

Since the Court’s ruling, several states have already made abortion illegal, denying thousands access to essential health care. As states rush to enforce their extreme abortion bans, the Center for Reproductive Rights and its partners continue to fight to block them and preserve access to care. (Source: Center for Reproductive Rights)
